Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo pork belly or pork shoulder, boiled and diced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 pieces green chili peppers, chopped
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat some cooking oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sliced onions, saut√© until they become fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the diced pork belly to the pot. Cook until it starts to brown and become crispy.
  3. Pour in the vinegar. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  4. Add the chopped green chili peppers, ground black pepper, and salt. Mix everything thoroughly.
  5. Continue cooking for another 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Adjust seasoning if necessary.
  6. Once everything is well-cooked and flavorful, remove from heat.
  7. Serve hot, traditionally on a sizzling plate, garnished with additional sliced onions or chili peppers if desired.
