Sisig

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo pork belly or pork shoulder, boiled and diced
  • 1/2 kilo pig's ears, boiled and diced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 pieces green chili peppers, chopped
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat some cooking oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onions, saut√© until they become fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the diced pork belly and pig's ears to the pan. Cook until they start to brown and become crispy.
  3. Pour in the soy sauce and vinegar. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  4. Add the chopped green chili peppers, ground black pepper, and salt. Mix everything thoroughly.
  5. Continue cooking for another 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Adjust seasoning if necessary.
  6. If desired, crack an egg over the sisig and stir it in until cooked for added richness.
  7. Once everything is well-cooked and flavorful, remove from heat.
  8. Serve hot, traditionally on a sizzling plate, garnished with additional chopped onions or chili peppers if desired.
